Material Design ripple effect on click. A circle expands from the click point and fades out, providing visual feedback for interactive elements.

Works on buttons, cards, list items — any clickable element.

### Options

- **color** — ripple color (default: currentColor)
- **opacity** — ripple opacity from 0 to 1 (default: 0.2)
- **duration** — animation duration in ms (default: 600)
- **centered** — always ripple from center instead of click point (default: false)
